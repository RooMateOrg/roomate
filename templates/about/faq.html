FAQs

Who are we and what do we do?

We are a service affiliated with NSBM Green University, dedicated to helping students find comfortable, convenient, and safe off-campus housing options.

Why should I use this website instead of finding housing myself?

We partner with reputable landlords to ensure a high-quality selection of listings.

Is this service affiliated with the university?

Yes, this website is a collaborative effort between NSBM Green University and dedicated housing professionals.

How can I contact a landlord about a listing?

Each listing provides a section to make a request to the landlord. You can also contact us for assistance.

Are there any fees associated with using this service?

No, this service is completely free to use.
